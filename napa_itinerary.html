Pfeiffer Beach
Scenic Stop
Sycamore Canyon Rd, Big Sur · $12 day use

🚗 ~15 min Pfeiffer Beach → Julia Pfeiffer Burns State Park
11:00 AM
Julia Pfeiffer Burns State Park
Hike
52801 CA-1, Big Sur · (831) 667-1112 · $10 day use
  • McWay Falls overlook — 80-ft waterfall dropping onto a cove beach
  • Ewoldsen Loop Trail: ~4 miles, 1,600 ft gain, dense redwoods + coastal views — best hike of the trip
  • Pay the entrance fee — rangers actively ticket non-payers
McWay Falls beach trail is closed for construction — viewable from the overlook above, still stunning. Ewoldsen loop is fully open.

1:30 PM
Nepenthe
Lunch
48510 CA-1, Big Sur · (831) 667-2345

🚗 ~35 min Nepenthe → Bixby Bridge + Garrapata State Park
2:30 PM
Bixby Bridge + Garrapata State Park
Hike + Views
34500 CA-1, Carmel · Free · Open 24 hrs
